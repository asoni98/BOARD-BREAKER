BOARD BREAKER TM
Created by Arun Soni
Kicking Animation made by Alejandro Hervella
Kung Fu Fighting Music by Carl Douglas

Instructions:
Two expert Taekwondo fighters duel for an epic battle lasting one minutes.
Their goal: to break the most boards in one minutes to become the Kung Fu Fighting Champion.

Controls:
Player 1:
↓ - moves down
↑ - moves up
← - moves left
→ - moves right

Player 2: wasd
Key S - moves down
Key W - moves up
Key A - moves left
Key D - moves right
